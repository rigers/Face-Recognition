Face Trainer      Face Recognizer

This simple web app utilizes OpenCV framework to analyze and recognize faces. In order for a face to be recognized the model needs to be trained. To train the model submit an image of a face and type a name. The more different images submited (i.e different facial expressions) the more accurate the model is at recognizing a face. To recognize a face upload a different image from the ones used to train the model and the app will give the results.
